what i do

I am a UI Designer & Developer transitioning into UX Design. I have 8 years of professional experience in Development, specializing in Front-End, with an educational background in Computer Science and Design (UX, Visual, and Graphic Design).

I work with Rio SEO (remotely in Seattle, WA) as a hybrid UI Designer and Senior Developer working on our Local Pages product. In addition to my daily tasks, I help support my team by bringing structure to our design process, creating standards for our project documentation, and building/maintaining our reusable and modular Sass code base.

I am currently most intrigued by Design Systems and Atomic Design.
