My Background

I found my passion at the intersection of tech and design, in User Experience Design. I've always felt that I use both sides of my brain pretty equally, but I let the left take over when I went down the path of a Computer Science degree and career in Development. For 9 years, I focused on building things with code until the need came for me to reawaken my right brain. I jumped at the chance to start working on User Interface Design at work and noticed my presentations were heavily weighted on the user's journey and experience. That was my light bulb! From there I learned more about User Experience and dove right in. I attended General Assembly's User Experience Design Circuit and SVC's Visual Design for UX and was able to tailor my role at work to focus heavily on Design work. I'm currently working with some of our largest clients through the UX process, while also working on honing in our Design process as a team.
