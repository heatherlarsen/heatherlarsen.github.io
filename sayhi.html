About Me

The dreaded "Hi, I’m [insert-name-here]" Here it goes...

The Generic Facts

Hi, I’m Heather Larsen. I'm a San Diego native...technically, an Alpine native. And no, I didn’t move to Seattle to work for Amazon. I moved to Seattle because I find it stunning. Seattle and it’s neighbors posses such beauty and after 4 years I’m still in awe and in love.

I have a BS in Computer Science from University of Redlands and a Certificate in JavaScript from University of Washington. I’ve also completed General Assembly’s UX Design Circuit program and School of Visual Concepts’s Visual Design for UX course.

The Design Facts

Design & creativity have always been a big part of my life. As a child, I was always crafting or coloring with my grandmother. In the days of MySpace, I taught myself HTML & CSS so I could customize my page. And in college, I took several computer based arts courses.

My love/passion for UX came when I realized my voice got louder when I was advocating for my users.

I am currently most intrigued by Design Systems, Atomic Design, and hand-off/technical documentation (read: consistency, modularity & scalability)
