About Me

The Generic Facts

Hi, I’m Heather Larsen. I'm a San Diego native (technically, an Alpine native), currently living in Seattle, WA. No, I didn’t move to Seattle to work for Amazon; I moved here because I find it absolutely stunning. Seattle and it’s neighbors possess such beauty and after 4 years I’m still in awe and in love.

I have a BS in Computer Science from University of Redlands and a Certificate in JavaScript from University of Washington. I’ve also completed General Assembly’s UX Design Circuit program and School of Visual Concepts’s Visual Design for UX course.

The Design Facts

Design & creativity have always been a big part of my life. As a child, I was constantly crafting or coloring with my grandmother. In the days of MySpace, I taught myself HTML & CSS so I could customize my page. And in college, I took several computer based arts courses.

My love and passion for UX stems from the work I was doing as a developer and the realization that my voice got louder and more confident when advocating for the users.

I am currently most intrigued by Design Systems, Atomic Design, and hand-off/technical documentation (read: consistency, modularity & scalability).

The Random Facts

I have quite a fondness for coffee shops that grew from my life as a remote employee. My current favorite for coffee is Caffe Umbria, for atmosphere is Fremont Coffee and for food is Ada’s Technical Books. And I’m always looking to explore more, so please send suggestions.

I am a repeat watcher of Parks & Recreation and Friends.

I, typically, make jokes about stealing puppies. I never would. Although I wouldn’t mind living on a large piece of land with all the puppies.

I am very content just sitting on a patio and watching the ocean.

Anyone know where I can find a good gluten-free/dairy-free cookie in Seattle? I'm on the hunt.
